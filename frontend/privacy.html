Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you register, such as your name, email, birth date, gender, location, interests, and profile photos. We also collect usage data and messages sent within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our app experience
  • To match you with other users
  • To send relevant notifications
  • To maintain account security and user safety

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted partners for hosting, analytics, or moderationâ€”but only as needed to run the app.

4. Your Rights

  • You can view and update your profile information anytime.
  • You can delete your account from the app.
  • You can contact us to request access or deletion of your data.

5. Security

We use encryption, secure hosting, and internal policies to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page and you'll be notified if they are significant.

7. Account Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and personal data at any time. To delete your account:

  1. Go to your Profile Settings
  2. Scroll down to the "Account" section
  3. Click on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

Alternatively, you can contact us at support@blendup.com to request account deletion. We will process your request within 30 days.

Please note that some information may be retained in our records for legal or administrative purposes, such as preventing fraud, resolving disputes, and enforcing our terms.

8. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us at privacy@blendup.com
