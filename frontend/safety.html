Your Safety Matters

We want you to have a safe and positive experience. Please follow these tips:

  • Protect your personal info: Never share your address, banking info, or other sensitive details too early.
  • Meet in public: Always meet in safe, public places on the first few dates.
  • Tell someone: Let a friend or family member know where you're going and who you're meeting.
  • Trust your instincts: If something feels off, you're probably right. Don't hesitate to block or report.
  • Don't send money: No matter the story, never send money to someone you met online.
  • Use our tools: You can block and report users easily through their profile or chat.

Report or Block

If someone is making you uncomfortable or violating our rules, please report them. We take all reports seriously and act quickly.
