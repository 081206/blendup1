About Blendup

Our Mission: To connect people in meaningful ways—whether it's for love, friendship, or new experiences.

We created Blendup because we believe dating should be fun, respectful, and safe. With features that put your security and preferences first, Blendup lets you focus on what really matters—getting to know the person on the other side of the screen.

What's Inside the App?

  • 📱 Sleek user interface designed for fast, fun, and intuitive swiping
  • ❤️ Smart Matchmaking: Get personalized suggestions based on your interests, preferences, and bio
  • 🔒 Verified Profiles: All users are reviewed to help reduce bots and fake accounts
  • 💬 Real-time Chat: Talk securely with your matches, with the option to report or block anytime
  • 🎥 Profile Videos & Reels: Show off your personality with short videos
  • 📊 Admin-reviewed Reports & Flags: Community safety is actively monitored
  • 🔔 Push Notifications: Get alerts when someone likes you or sends a message
  • 🎯 Privacy Settings: Full control over who sees your profile, and what they see

What Makes Us Different?

  • ✅ Verified user profiles to prevent fake accounts
  • ✅ Swipe-based discovery that's fast and intuitive
  • ✅ Smart matching based on your profile and interests
  • ✅ Focus on safety and privacy at every step

Our Values

  • 🤝 Respect First – Everyone deserves to feel safe and valued
  • 📶 Real Connections – It's not about numbers, it's about people
  • 🛠️ Built for You – We listen and improve based on your feedback
