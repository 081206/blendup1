Our Story

FindYourMatch began as a passion project by a group of friends who were tired of dating apps that felt more like games than relationship tools. We decided to build a platform focused on honesty, safety, and human-first features.

Our mission is to create a dating experience where people can be their true selves, where safety is a priority, and where real connections can flourish. We believe that technology should enhance human relationships, not complicate them.

FindYourMatch Launches to Redefine Safe and Genuine Online Dating

May 15, 2025 ‚Äì Remote, Worldwide

FindYourMatch, a next-generation dating app, officially launches to help people build authentic relationships with a safety-first approach. With features like verified profiles, block/report tools, and interest-based matching, FindYourMatch is redefining how people meet online.

"We wanted to create a space where people feel safe being themselves," says the FindYourMatch team. "Unlike traditional dating apps that focus on speed or swiping, we prioritize real connections and safety."

The app is currently available on the web and Android, with an iOS version coming soon.
